Saugat Siwakoti March 11, 2026
Since the release of OpenClaw, there has been significant buzz around using AI agents for basic administrative hygiene like email and calendar management. However, this narrow focus vastly underutilizes the platform's potential. To achieve maximum ROI, we should look at deploying OpenClaw on robust infrastructure (such as AWS) and equipping it with specialized skills for high-volume, repetitive workflows like research and content review. Jason Calacanis recently detailed his organization's success with this approach on the All-In Podcast, demonstrating how a properly architected OpenClaw setup can function as a high-value digital workforce.
