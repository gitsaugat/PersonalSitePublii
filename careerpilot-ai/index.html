CareerPilot AI

Saugat Siwakoti March 11, 2026

The job market is competitive.
Most candidates are optimizing blindly.

So I built a system that removes the blind spots. 🚀

CareerPilot AI analyzes how well your resume aligns with a specific job description — before you submit it.

No guessing.
No endless rewriting loops.
No hoping you pass ATS filters. ❌

With CareerPilot AI, you can:

• Compare multiple resume versions instantly
• Generate a tailored, optimized resume
• Improve ATS alignment with actionable insights 📊
• Auto-generate role-specific cover letters ✍️
• Get targeted interview prep guidance 🎯
• Optimize your LinkedIn strategically

Built using LLMs through Ollama (DeepSeek + open-source models), the platform gives users control, flexibility, and performance — without heavy reliance on closed APIs.

It started as a personal productivity tool.

It’s evolving into something much bigger. 🔥
